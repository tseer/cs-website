Services Computing Laboratory (SCLab) focuses on developing methods and algorithms for services selection and recommendation. Specifically, SCLab focuses on capturing and including users' evolving preferences in machine and deep learning algorithms to improve the accuracy of services selection and recommendation. Areas of research include exploring user temporal and contextual preferences from sequential actions, detecting fake likers in social media networks, and improving recommendation accuracy with knowledge graphs.
