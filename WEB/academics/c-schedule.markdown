Course offerings now appear in the dynamic [Course Catalog]({{ '/academics/course-catalog/' | relative_url }}).

The catalog is generated from local `schedule_*.xlsx` files through the course offerings import pipeline and includes the current academic year plus future academic years when schedule files exist.
